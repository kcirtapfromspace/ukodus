Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

Ukodus is a Sudoku puzzle game developed by Patrick Deutsch. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information across the web app at ukodus.now and the iOS app.

Web Version

When playing on ukodus.now, the following applies:

Anonymous Gameplay Data

Game results—puzzle data, solve time, mistakes, and hints used—are submitted to the server for leaderboard and puzzle analytics. No personal information is attached.

Player ID

A randomly generated player ID is stored in your browser's localStorage. It is never linked to your identity, email, or any account. No sign-up is required.

Move Log

Move actions are recorded with timestamps for anti-cheat verification. This data is used solely to validate leaderboard submissions.

No Accounts Needed

You can play, compete on the leaderboard, and share puzzles without creating an account. Everything works anonymously.

iOS App

All game data in the iOS app is stored locally on your device: game progress, saved games, statistics, and preferences.

Apple Game Center

If you choose to sign in to Game Center, your scores may appear on leaderboards and achievements sync with your Game Center account. This data is managed by Apple according to their privacy policy.

Apple iCloud

If you enable iCloud sync, game progress syncs across your Apple devices. Data is stored in your personal iCloud account, managed by Apple according to their privacy policy.

What We Do NOT Collect

Ukodus does not collect any of the following, on any platform:

No Personal Info

No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact information of any kind.

No Location Data

We never request or store your geographic location, IP-based geolocation, or any location identifiers.

No Ads or Trackers

No advertising networks, no tracking pixels, no analytics SDKs, no advertising identifiers (IDFA). Zero third-party services.

Children's Privacy

Ukodus does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. The app contains no user-generated content, social features, or in-app purchases.

Data Security

All local data is protected by your device's built-in security measures. Web gameplay data is transmitted over HTTPS. iCloud data is protected by Apple's security infrastructure. We do not store passwords or sensitive credentials because no accounts exist.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Your Rights

You can delete all app data at any time by:

Delete the App

Removing the iOS app from your device deletes all locally stored game data, statistics, and preferences.

Reset Statistics

Use the "Reset Statistics" option in Settings to clear your play history while keeping the app installed.

Clear Browser Data

For the web version, clear your browser's localStorage for ukodus.now to remove your anonymous player ID and saved game state.
